Accessibility

Puzzly is trying to make the website as accessible as possible. The website is designed mostly for children so we are trying to avoid using volcaburary which could be difficult to understand. In addition to this, we are using font colors which are clearly visible on the background. Not everybody has Desktops/Smartphones so we made our website accessible for everyone who has either a smartphone, a tablet or a desktop. Moreover, we are avoiding small fonts so that everyone with not perfect sight could use our website.
